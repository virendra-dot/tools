How to Compress Images Online

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking "Select Files"
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider
  3. Choose your desired output format (or keep original)
  4. Click "Compress Images" to process your files
  5. Download the optimized images individually or all at once

Why Compress Your Images?

Image compression helps:

  • Reduce bandwidth usage and storage costs
  • Enhance user experience with faster page loads
  • Meet platform requirements for social media and email
  • Optimize images for search engine visibility
